# Finding Common Ground in AGI Strategy Debates

> Source: https://www.rand.org/pubs/perspectives/PEA4448-1.html
> Collected: 2026-05-11
> Published: 2026-03-09

This RAND perspective argues that two interdependent prerequisites are needed to realize AI benefits: a human-AI coexistence ecosystem in which humans retain meaningful agency and a global security architecture stable enough to make that ecosystem possible.
