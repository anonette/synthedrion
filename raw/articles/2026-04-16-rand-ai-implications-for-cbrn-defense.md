# AI Implications for Chemical, Biological, Radiological, and Nuclear Defense Policy and Programs

> Source: https://www.rand.org/pubs/perspectives/PEA4611-1.html
> Collected: 2026-05-11
> Published: 2026-04-16

This RAND perspective argues that AI is reshaping the CBRN risk landscape by enhancing detection, decisionmaking, and crisis response while also enabling more sophisticated and destabilizing capabilities for state and non-state actors. It presents a research agenda centered on risk assessment, crisis management, deterrence, and cross-sector collaboration in an era of U.S.-China technological rivalry.
