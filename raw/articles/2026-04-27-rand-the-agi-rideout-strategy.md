# The AGI Rideout Strategy for Reducing Strategic Risk and Promoting Stability in the Transition to Artificial General Intelligence

> Source: https://www.rand.org/pubs/perspectives/PEA4347-1.html
> Collected: 2026-05-11
> Published: 2026-04-27

This RAND perspective argues that the United States should prioritize an AGI Rideout strategy focused on reducing strategic disaster risk, deterring AI-fueled conflict, and increasing the resilience of the AI ecosystem rather than trying simply to win an all-out AGI race.
