# Open-Weight AI Models Require Proportional Evaluation Approaches

> Source: https://www.rand.org/pubs/perspectives/PEA4886-1.html
> Collected: 2026-05-11
> Published: 2026-05-04

This RAND perspective argues that open-weight AI models create distinct risk factors that existing evaluation practices, designed largely for closed-weight model deployment, fail to capture. The authors propose proportional evaluation approaches and find that most model families released in 2025 through April 2026 did not meet those standards.
