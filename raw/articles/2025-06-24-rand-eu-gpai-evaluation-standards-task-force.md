# Toward Best Practices for AI Evaluation and Governance

> Source: https://www.rand.org/pubs/perspectives/PEA3624-1.html
> Collected: 2026-05-11
> Published: 2025-06-24

This RAND perspective argues that the European Union, as the first jurisdiction to mandate evaluations for general-purpose AI models presenting systemic risk, should establish a dedicated GPAI Evaluation Standards Task Force. It identifies four desiderata for evaluations: internal validity, external validity, reproducibility, and portability.

The source is useful because it gives the EU folder a direct governance model centered on evaluation quality, institutional design, and implementation of the AI Act rather than on pure frontier-race competition.
