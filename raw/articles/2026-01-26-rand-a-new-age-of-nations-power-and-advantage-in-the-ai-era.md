# A New Age of Nations: Power and Advantage in the AI Era

> Source: https://www.rand.org/pubs/perspectives/PEA3691-14.html
> Collected: 2026-05-11
> Published: 2026-01-26

This RAND perspective argues that the competitive challenge of AI is primarily social rather than merely technological. It frames AI as a general-purpose technology that will reshape national power and argues that societies that become more competitive overall, not just those with the best models, will gain long-term advantage.
