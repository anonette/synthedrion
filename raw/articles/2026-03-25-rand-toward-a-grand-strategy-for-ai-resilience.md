# Toward a Grand Strategy for AI Resilience

> Source: https://www.rand.org/pubs/perspectives/PEA4525-1.html
> Collected: 2026-05-11
> Published: 2026-03-25

This RAND perspective argues that resilience should be the central public-policy goal in the face of AI-driven societal transformation. It contrasts resilience with both do-nothing and wait-and-see approaches and frames resilience as the ability of societies and institutions to withstand shocks, adapt, and recover.
