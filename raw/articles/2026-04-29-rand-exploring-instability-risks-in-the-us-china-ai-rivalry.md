# Exploring Instability Risks in the U.S.-China AI Rivalry

> Source: https://www.rand.org/pubs/perspectives/PEA4703-1.html
> Collected: 2026-05-11
> Published: 2026-04-29

This RAND perspective presents Breakwater, a game designed to explore instability risks in the U.S.-China rivalry and race to AGI. It focuses on conditions under which competitors might take preventive action such as cyberattacks or military force against elements of a rival AI ecosystem and on what stabilizes or destabilizes the competition.
