# Nvidia Says Its Chips Have No Backdoors After China Flags H20 Security Concerns

> Source: https://www.reuters.com/world/china/nvidia-says-its-chips-have-no-backdoors-after-china-flags-h20-security-concerns-2025-07-31/
> Collected: 2026-05-11
> Published: 2025-07-31

Reuters reports that China's Cyberspace Administration raised concerns over whether Nvidia's H20 AI chips could contain backdoor security risks after U.S. proposals to require tracking and location-verification functions on advanced chips sold abroad. Nvidia responded that its products have no backdoors that would allow remote access or control.

The article matters because it shows China turning chip-security concerns into a sovereignty issue linked to data, privacy, and foreign controllability. It also suggests that even where China still wants or needs foreign chips, it is willing to frame those chips as security liabilities if they preserve U.S. technical leverage.

The source also notes that Chinese authorities have previously used security-risk arguments against foreign chipmakers and that analysts see China as increasingly confident in domestic substitution, even if near-term demand for Nvidia chips remains strong.
