# Contingency Frameworks for Future U.S.-China Cooperation on AI Assurance and Security

> Source: https://www.rand.org/pubs/perspectives/PEA4825-1.html
> Collected: 2026-05-11
> Published: 2026-05-04

This RAND perspective argues that even if bilateral U.S.-China AI cooperation is infeasible in the near term, policymakers should preserve strategic optionality for future cooperation on AI assurance and security. It outlines five lines of effort that could support a more stable and predictable international environment for AI development and deployment if conditions change.
