# RAND Response to NIST Request for Input on AI 100-5, A Plan for Global Engagement on AI Standards

> Source: https://www.rand.org/pubs/perspectives/PEA3379-2.html
> Collected: 2026-05-11
> Published: 2025-03-03

This RAND perspective responds to a NIST request for input on global engagement on AI standards. It discusses prioritization of standardization topics, activities, missing ideas, and the completeness of standards work.

Although the source is U.S.-facing, it is highly relevant to the EU folder because it reinforces the importance of standards, technical governance, and international legitimacy as strategic levers in AI competition.
