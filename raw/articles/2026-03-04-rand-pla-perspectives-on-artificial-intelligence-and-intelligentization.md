# The People's Liberation Army's Perspectives on Artificial Intelligence Highlighting Integration as Key to Intelligentization Goals

> Source: https://www.rand.org/pubs/perspectives/PEA4574-1.html
> Collected: 2026-05-11
> Published: 2026-03-04

This RAND perspective reviews Chinese military journals, media, and officer statements to analyze how the PLA sees AI as central to intelligentization. It highlights integration across components, cross-domain information integration, multiagent collaboration, data transmission, and logistics, while noting potential vulnerabilities and implementation challenges.
