# Open Models, Soft Power, and the Spectrum of U.S.-China Artificial Intelligence Competition

> Source: https://www.rand.org/pubs/perspectives/PEA4686-1.html
> Collected: 2026-05-11
> Published: 2026-03-26

This RAND perspective argues that U.S.-China AI competition is increasingly about shaping the global AI ecosystem and that open models are becoming a major instrument of soft power. It compares U.S. and Chinese action plans and argues that support for open model ecosystems, revised export controls, and permissive licensing matter to geopolitical influence.
