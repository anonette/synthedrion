# Measuring Biological Capabilities and Risks of AI Agents

> Source: https://www.rand.org/pubs/perspectives/PEA4710-1.html
> Collected: 2026-05-11
> Published: 2026-02-10

This RAND perspective examines biological agentic evaluations as a tool for assessing the capabilities and risks of increasingly autonomous AI systems. It emphasizes how evaluation design choices shape conclusions and policy relevance.
