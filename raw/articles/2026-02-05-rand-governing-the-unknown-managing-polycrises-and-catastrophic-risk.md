# Governing the Unknown

> Source: https://www.rand.org/pubs/perspectives/PEA4610-1.html
> Collected: 2026-05-11
> Published: 2026-02-05

This RAND perspective summarizes workshop insights on managing polycrises and catastrophic risk. It highlights AI-powered early warning systems, regional data consortia, resilience investment trusts, and innovative insurance solutions as adaptation strategies.
