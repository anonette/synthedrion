# RAND Europe's Response to the EU Cloud and AI Development Act Call for Evidence

> Source: https://www.rand.org/pubs/testimonies/CTA4156-1.html
> Collected: 2026-05-11
> Published: 2025-07-09

This RAND Europe response addresses the European Commission's proposed EU Cloud and AI Development Act. It focuses on Europe's leadership in high-performance computing resources and digital infrastructure for AI development and offers strategic recommendations for an EU compute strategy.

This source is important because it moves the EU corpus beyond abstract standards talk and into infrastructure, cloud, compute strategy, and industrial policy design.
