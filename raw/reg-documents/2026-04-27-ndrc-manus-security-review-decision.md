# NDRC Security Review Decision on the Manus Acquisition

> Source: https://zfxxgk.ndrc.gov.cn/web/iteminfo.jsp?id=20623
> Collected: 2026-05-11
> Published: 2026-04-27

The Office of the Foreign Investment Security Review Working Mechanism at the National Development and Reform Commission announced that it had made a prohibition decision on the foreign acquisition of Manus and required the parties to revoke the acquisition transaction.

Although brief, the notice is important because it establishes an official precedent that a strategically sensitive AI acquisition can be blocked and unwound on foreign investment security review grounds.
