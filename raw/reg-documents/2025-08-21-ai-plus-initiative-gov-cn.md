# Opinions of the State Council on Deepening the Implementation of the "Artificial Intelligence+" Initiative

> Source: https://www.gov.cn/zhengce/content/202508/content_7037861.htm
> Collected: 2026-05-11
> Published: 2025-08-21

State Council [2025] Document No. 11

This State Council opinion calls for deep implementation of the `Artificial Intelligence+` initiative to promote broad and deep integration of AI across the economy and society, reshape production and living, accelerate the formation of a smart economy and smart society, and better serve Chinese-style modernization.

## Overall Requirements

Guided by Xi Jinping Thought on Socialism with Chinese Characteristics for a New Era, the document stresses people-centered development, forward-looking planning, systematic deployment, sector-specific implementation, openness and sharing, and security and controllability.

It focuses on science and technology, industry, consumption, livelihoods, governance, and global cooperation, while highlighting China's advantages in data resources, industrial completeness, and broad application scenarios.

Targets include:

- By 2027: broad and deep integration in six key fields, more than 70 percent penetration of smart terminals and intelligent agents, stronger public-governance use, and a more developed open-cooperation system.
- By 2030: more than 90 percent penetration of smart terminals and intelligent agents, AI as a major growth pole, and broad empowerment of high-quality development.
- By 2035: full entry into a new stage of intelligent economy and smart society development.

## Key Initiatives

The opinion organizes AI rollout across:

- science and technology
- industrial development
- consumption upgrading
- livelihoods and well-being
- governance capacity
- global cooperation

It supports AI-driven scientific discovery, industrial intelligentization, agricultural modernization, service innovation, smart consumer products, AI in education and health, AI-enabled public governance, and international cooperation including support for Global South AI capacity-building.

## Basic Support Capabilities

The document also emphasizes:

- model capability improvement
- high-quality data supply
- national intelligent compute coordination
- application pilot bases and service chains
- open-source ecosystem construction
- talent development
- legal, regulatory, and ethics frameworks
- stronger safety, monitoring, warning, and response capacity

## Implementation Logic

The final section explicitly states that Party leadership should run through the entire `Artificial Intelligence+` process, with the National Development and Reform Commission coordinating implementation and local authorities adapting policies to actual conditions.

This source is the canonical Chinese primary-policy anchor for the `AI+` layer in this project.
