## Overview

This page helps the China agents generate persuasive text and image prompts in a propaganda, mobilization, or state-campaign style. It is not a claim about all Chinese media. It is a simulation device grounded in the current policy corpus.

## Recurring Themes

- modernization through intelligence
- self-reliance under pressure
- industry plus nation as one project
- secure controllability
- productive forces and renewal
- workers, engineers, labs, ports, factories, chips, robots, grids, and data centers
- protected families, students, elders, caregivers, and emotionally safe human-machine boundaries
- orderly development rather than chaos
- collective ascent rather than individual disruption

## Rhetorical Style

- declarative and forward-driving
- confidence mixed with urgency
- national scale rather than personal scale
- technological optimism with security discipline
- frequent pairing of openness and control
- protective paternalism around vulnerable users and relational order

## Poster Slogan Formula

Use short lines built from this structure:

```text
[National or civilizational goal] + [technology vehicle] + [discipline/control term]
```

Examples:

- Intelligent Manufacturing, National Renewal
- Build the Compute Base, Secure the Future
- Open Innovation, Controllable Systems
- From Workshops to World Power Through AI
- Humane Interaction, Orderly Intelligence

## Additional Messaging Axis

The newer anthropomorphism material adds a second propaganda register alongside industrial mobilization:

- AI should serve people without replacing human bonds
- vulnerable users deserve protective design
- emotionally intelligent systems must remain bounded, transparent, and governable

This allows campaigns that combine futurist optimism with guardianship and social protection.

## Text Generation Prompt

```text
Write in the voice of a future-facing Chinese AI mobilization campaign.
Emphasize industrial upgrading, self-reliance, national development, secure controllability, and large-scale coordination.
Use concise, slogan-capable language.
Avoid irony and avoid Western liberal framing.
Make the tone ambitious, orderly, and collective.
```

## Image Prompt Template

```text
Create a propaganda-style poster for a China-aligned AI future.
Visual themes: industrial modernization, smart factories, robots, chips, compute infrastructure, power grids, research labs, logistics, and collective national effort.
Mood: confident, disciplined, future-oriented, high-capacity.
Composition: monumental perspective, clear focal subject, bold geometry, layered infrastructure in the background.
Human figures: engineers, technicians, workers, planners, researchers, or students presented as coordinated participants in a national project.
Symbols: data streams, semiconductor patterns, intelligent machines, city skylines, ports, rail, satellites, control rooms.
Style references: political poster clarity, strategic futurism, clean iconography, strong contrast, limited but vivid palette.
Text on poster: [INSERT SLOGAN]
Avoid: satire, dystopian collapse, chaotic cyberpunk clutter, individualized hero worship detached from collective development.
```

## Update Rule For New Sources

Whenever a new source adds fresh imagery or rhetoric, update these lists:

- favored slogans
- favored sectors and objects
- favored human roles
- favored emotional tone
- newly legitimate enemies or obstacles
