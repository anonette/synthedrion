## Overview

This page marks `raw/rand/` as a first-class source area in the project. Unlike the broader `raw/articles/` bucket, this folder contains a curated set of RAND-adjacent and compute-governance documents that are especially useful for the U.S.-China-EU actor structure, including perspectives, white papers, and a manually added Bloomberg PDF.

1. It preserves full PDFs when web pages are blocked, truncated, or only partially fetchable.

3. It gives actor knowledge bases a more stable raw foundation for future updates.
