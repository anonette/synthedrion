## Current U.S. Corpus Shape

The present U.S. material emphasizes:

- strategic stability and conflict avoidance in AI rivalry
- resilience as a public-policy goal
- open models as soft-power instruments
- cloud-based compute control as a strategic alternative to unrestricted chip export
- selective nontechnical engagement with China on AI safety and governance
- preservation of optionality for future U.S.-China cooperation
- national advantage as a broad social and institutional problem, not only a model race
- compute providers as regulatory intermediaries and global compute partnerships as instruments of U.S. leadership
